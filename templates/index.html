How the pipeline thinks

From raw text to a confident verdict in five intelligent steps.

01

Clean

HTML, noise and stopwords are stripped; words are lemmatised to their root form.

02

Vectorise

TF-IDF converts the review into a 10,000-feature numeric fingerprint.

03

Predict

Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression and SVM each cast an independent vote.

04

Read tone

TextBlob reads the raw emotional polarity straight from the language.

05

Decide

A majority vote fuses every signal into one confident final verdict.
